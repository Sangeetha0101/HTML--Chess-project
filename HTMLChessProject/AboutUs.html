About Us

Welcome to Black Purple Chess Academy, your premier destination for comprehensive chess education and training. Founded in 2021, we are dedicated to nurturing a passion for chess and empowering students of all ages and skill levels to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

At Black Purple Chess Academy, our mission is to promote the love of chess and foster a supportive learning environment where students can develop critical thinking skills, enhance their strategic abilities, and build confidence both on and off the chessboard.

Our Approach

We believe in a holistic approach to chess education that combines expert instruction, personalized guidance, and a love for the game. Our team of experienced chess coaches and instructors are passionate about sharing their knowledge and expertise with students, helping them unlock their innate talents and achieve their goals.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Experienced Coaches: Our team consists of professional chess masters and educators with years of experience.
  • Personalized Instruction: We offer personalized instruction tailored to each student's needs and learning style.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: We cover all aspects of chess, from basic rules to advanced strategies and tournament preparation.
  • Supportive Community: We foster a welcoming community where students can connect, compete, and grow together.

Our Programs

Whether you're a beginner or an advanced player, we offer a range of programs to suit your needs.

Packages

  • Beginner's Training: Learn the rules, basic tactics, and strategies.
  • Intermediate Training: Advanced lessons on opening theory, middlegame strategies, and endgame techniques.
  • Advanced Training: Intensive sessions, personalized coaching, and tournament preparation.
